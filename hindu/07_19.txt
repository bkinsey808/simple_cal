Karaka Chaturthi, Karva Chaut: married women fast for the entire day for the longevity, prosperty and well being of their husbands.  One-day festival celebrated by Hindu and some Sikh women in North India and parts of Pakistan in which married women fast from sunrise to moonrise for the safety and longevity of their husbands. Sometimes, unmarried women observe the fast for their fiances or desired husbands.
