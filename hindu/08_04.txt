Nahakha: On the first day of Chhath Puja, the devotees take a dip, preferably in the holy river Ganga and carry home the holy water of the river Ganga water to prepare the offerings. The house and surroundings are scrupulously cleaned. The pavitran allows herself/himself only one meal on this day.

Vaikuntha-chaturdashi:

Karthika Suddha Chavithi: Naga puja and Subrahmanyeswara puja will bring longevity and health
