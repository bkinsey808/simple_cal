Karthika Bahula Ekadashi, Utpanna Ekadashi: considered the birth anniversary of Ekadashi. Goddess Ekadashi who is one of the Shaktis of Lord Vishnu. Ekadashi was born out of Lord Vishnu to annihilate Demon Mur who intended to kill sleeping Lord Vishnu. Hence Goddess Ekadashi is one of the protective powers of Lord Vishnu. Devotees, who pledge to observe yearly fasting, begin Ekadashi fasting from Utpanna Ekadashi. Kubera puja brings money.
