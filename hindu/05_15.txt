Raksha Bandhan: festival celebrated mainly in northern Indian states. Rakhi is a special occasion to celebrate the chaste bond of love between a brother and a sister.

Rig Yajuh Sravana: For students of the Rigveda and Yajurveda, the chief time of renewing the sacred thread
Rakhi Purnima: refers to the tying of a piece of silk or string around the arm as a preservative against evil spirits.

Narati-purnima: coconuts are thrown into the sea, and the monsoon is declared broken up

Hayagriva Jayanti: birth of Hayagriva
