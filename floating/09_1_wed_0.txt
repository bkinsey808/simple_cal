Fight Procrastination Day: Putting things off -- we've all been guilty of it. You don't like doing the job at hand, the task seems OVERWHELMING, you're a perfectionist, you don't know where to begin, the job seems scary, you are afraid of failing -- there are lots of reasons for PROCRASTINATING. You can't make yourself do something you hate, but you can learn not to hate the job quite as much. Overcoming procrastination is less about developing will-power, and more about creating an environment that is CONDUCIVE to completing the task at hand. So let's take a moment this month to look at some ways you can curb your tendency to procrastinate.
