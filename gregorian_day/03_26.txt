Prince Jonah Kuhio Kalanianaole Day: {Hawaii} Prince Kūhiō Day is an official holiday in the state of Hawaiʻi in the United States. It is celebrated annually on March 26, to mark the birth of Prince Jonah Kūhiō Kalanianaʻole — heir to the throne of the Kingdom of Hawaiʻi, prince of the House of Kalākaua, and later territorial delegate to the United States Congress. In 1895, at the age of twenty four, he participated in a rebellion against the Republic of Hawaiʻi.
