Sementivae: {Roman} Festival of Sowing, in honor of Tellus (Mother Earth) and Ceres. Also a festival of peace since Ceres was nursed by Pax (Peace) and is Her foster child. Folk may hang oscilla (little swinging figures for protection) in the trees.
