Blood Banks Day: Blood Banks Day is probably initially linked to the Catholic and Eastern Orthodox Christian celebrations of St. Januarius' Day. St. Januarius was a 3rd Century Italian bishop and martyr, who has become known as the patron saint of blood banks.

"Holy _____, Batman" Day: recalling the repeated catchphrase of Batman's sidekick Robin (played by Burt Ward in the 1960s ABC Batman television series).
