Septimontia: {Roman} This festival was held to honor the Seven Hills of Rome, either all of them together, or just the earliest enclosed part (inside the Servian Wall, which enclosed parts of the Caelian, Esquiline, Palatine, and Velian hills). During the festival sacrifices were made somewhere on each hill.

Agonalia III: {Roman} Another day sacred to Janus, the bookend to that of January 9. Later also a day sacred to Sol Indiges.
