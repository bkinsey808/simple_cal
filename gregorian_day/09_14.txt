National Anthem Day: {US} marks the date when Francis Scott Key actually wrote the lyrics to "The Star-Spangled Banner," America's national anthem. After enduring the British attack on Maryland's Fort McHenry on September 14, 1814, Francis Scott Key wrote his most famous lines.
