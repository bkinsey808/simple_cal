Chant Day: Chant Day probably began with the celebration of St. Gregory the Great (Pope Gregory I), a Catholic saint and papal father credited with the invention of the Gregorian Chant. St. Gregory the Great is traditionally known as the patron saint of music, singers, schoolchildren, teachers and the plague.
