Declaration of the Bab: {Bahai} The Baha’i commemorates the day in 1844, when the Bab, the herald of the Baha’i Faith, announced in Shiraz, Persia (now Iran), that he was the herald of a new messenger of God. It is one of the nine holy days of the year when work is suspended.

Tubilustrium II: {Roman} To Vulcan, who is responsible for the making of the sacred war trumpets (tubas). 

Birthday of Margaret Fuller: {Unitarian} Transcendentalist and feminist who argued that women, as well as men, have immortal souls.
