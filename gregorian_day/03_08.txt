International Women's Day: {social justice} International Women's Day (IWD) is marked on the 8th of March  every year. It is a major day of global celebration of women. In different regions the focus of the celebrations ranges from general celebration of respect, appreciation and love towards women to a celebration for women's economic, political and social achievements. Day to mourn victims of gender-based oppression and misogyny (past and present), make peace, and celebrate women's empowerment.
