Epulum Iovis, Feast of Jupiter: {Roman} This feast was attended by senators and high ranking magistrates during the Ludi Romani and Ludi Plebeii. It started with the sacrifice of a white cow and ritual cakes to honor Jupiter, and possibly Juno and Minerva.
