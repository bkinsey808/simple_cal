World Peace Day: {Peace} encourages people to be kind, peaceful and teach peace by example. Started by Don Morris "Peaceguy" of Miami, Florida around 1997. Not to be confused with International Day of Peace, or Give Peace a Chance Day.

International Students' Day: an international observance of student community, originally in remembrance of the Nazi German storming of Czech universities in 1939 and the subsequent killing and sending of students to concentration camps.
