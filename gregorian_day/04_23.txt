Vinalia Priora: {Roman} "First Festival of the Vine". On this day the first wines of the year were tasted, and libations were made in honour of Jupiter. It was also a special day for prostitutes, who payed homage to Venus.

Vinalia Urbana: {Roman} To Jupiter and Venus. Important for vine growers, probably corresponding to the critical time of the first vine shoots and their possible exposure to killing frost.
