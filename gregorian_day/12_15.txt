Bill of Rights Day: {US} On September 25, 1789, the First Congress of the United States proposed to the state legislatures 12 amendments to the Constitution. 10 of these amendments were added to the Constitution on December 15, 1791. 

Consualia III: {Roman} To Consus, god of Time, whose presence is obviously connected with the end of the year. This is the start of the Halcyon Days, the seven days preceding and the seven days following the Winter Solstice.
