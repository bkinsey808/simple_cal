Bloodiest Day: {US} On September 17, 1862, during the Civil War, this date marked a gruesome battle. Union forces repelled a Confederate attack in Maryland, fighting the Battle of Antietam, on this date. More than 23,100 soldiers were captured, wounded or slain in the conflict, which has become known as the bloodiest day in American military history.
